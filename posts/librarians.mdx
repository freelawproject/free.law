<p class="lead">We have compiled a list of our ever-expanding features and capabilities in CourtListener. We hope librarians, researchers, educators, and other users find this resource helpful.</p>

## Advanced Research

<p className="lead">CourtListener is an advanced platform that supports complex research workflows across a vast corpus.</p>

- **Keyword Search** with advanced query techniques and operators support highly advanced Boolean queries across case law, PACER data, and oral argument transcripts.

  [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/search-operators/)

- **Semantic Search** lets you search case law using natural language. Instead of exact keyword matching, it finds relevant opinions based on the meaning of your query. Hybrid search lets you combine natural language with specific keywords by wrapping words or phrases in quotation marks.

  [Blog](/2026/05/04/semantic-search-on-courtlistener)

- **Citation Search** lets you look at all the opinions or filings that cite an opinion of interest and then filter those results so that you only see case law citing that opinion from particular jurisdictions, dates, keywords, etc. For example: "All the cases that site *Miranda v. Arizona* from the Fifth Circuit that mention drugs in the last five years."

  [Blog](/2016/01/30/citation-searching/)

- **Our MCP Server** can be integrated into AI tools like Claude and ChatGPT to ground topics and to empower the agents to research on your behalf.

  [Blog](/2026/05/12/courtlistener-mcp-ai-assistants/) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Help Page](https://wiki.free.law/c/courtlistener/help/api/mcp)


## Alerting

<p className="lead">CourtListener began as an legal awareness platform (hence the name) and sends millions of email alerts each year.</p>


- **Opinion Alerts** allow users to be notified when new opinions match their search terms and filters.

  [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/alerts/#search-alerts)

- **Citation Alerts** make it possible to keep up with citations to a case of interest.

  [Blog](/2020/02/20/citation-alerts/) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/alerts/#citation-alerts)

- **Docket Alerts** allow users to be notified when there is an update to a particular docket. We send thousands of docket alerts per day. 

  [Blog](/2018/08/21/announcing-pacer-docket-alerts-for-journalists-lawyers-researchers-and-the-public/) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/alerts/#recap-alerts)

- **Search Alerts** allow users to follow topics, people, organizations, types of cases—anything you can search for, you can monitor.

  [Blog](/2025/06/18/recap-search-alerts-for-pacer) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Demo Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jh9wbxMZGe4&t=1s) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/alerts/#search-alerts)

- **Relative date queries** can be used to keep searches and alerts dynamically up to date. 

  [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/relative-dates/)


## Case Law

<p className="lead">This is our collection of legal decisions. Courtlistener has one of the most comprehensive collections of American Legal Jurisprudence on the Internet.</p>
  
[Blog](/2024/03/26/all-the-case-law/) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Data Coverage](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/coverage/opinions/) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Try it Out](https://www.courtlistener.com/opinion/)

- **We have** nearly ten million legal decisions dating from 1650's to today across state, federal, and tribal courts, with updates coming in hourly.

  [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/coverage/opinions/)

- **We are scanning** millions of pages of case law directly from the books to keep our content up to date.

  [Blog](/2026/04/16/scanning-americas-case-law/)

- **Parenthetical** summaries of cases, written by judges, are available for many opinions. These contain the holdings of nearly every important case.

  [Blog](/2022/03/17/summarizing-important-cases/)

- **Notes** can be added to opinions to keep track of your thoughts about them.

  [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/tags-notes/#notes)


## RECAP Suite

<p className="lead">The RECAP Archive in CourtListener is the biggest open collection of federal court data on the internet.</p>

[About](/recap/) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Blog](/2023/03/13/what-is-in-the-recap-suite) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Data Coverage](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/coverage/recap/) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Try it Out](https://www.courtlistener.com/recap/)

- **RECAP Extension**, used by over 30,000 people, is a private and secure way to contribute your PACER purchases to CourtListener and to freely access anything somebody else has added to the archive. 

  [About](/recap) <img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" /> [Blog](/2023/03/13/what-is-in-the-recap-suite)

- **The RECAP Archive** is a fully searchable database of tens of millions of PACER cases and hundreds of millions of docket entries. 

  [Demo Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L9KG59MbedU)

- **RSS Feeds** are available for dockets and RECAP search if you prefer to receive your updates via RSS rather than email.

  [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/feeds/)

- **Pray and Pay** is a system so you can "Pray" for PACER documents you want, and others can grant those prayers by "Paying" for them on your behalf.

  [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/pray-and-pay/)

- **Tags** allow you to organize and describe the docket.

  [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/tags-notes/#tags)

- **Notes** can be added to RECAP dockets and documents.

  [Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/tags-notes/#notes)

- **@recap.email** is a better version of your PACER notification emails. Your emails become more readable and searchable, with quick access to free RECAP content. Any filings linked in your PACER alerts are automatically added to RECAP.

  [Blog](/2022/08/26/personal-recap-dot-email-addresses/)<img src="/images/icons/flp-node.svg" alt="" className="inline not-prose mx-1"  role="presentation" />[Help Page](https://www.courtlistener.com/help/recap/email/)
